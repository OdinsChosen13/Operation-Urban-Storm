FACTION OVERVIEW

Insurgent Forces are irregular fighters who overwhelm enemies through numbers, stress, and uncertainty. They force collapse through pressure, tempo disruption, and area denial. Their cheapness is their weapon.

FACTION TRAITS

1

Asymmetric Force Ratio

Insurgent models are among the cheapest in the game. Insurgents are expected to outnumber opponents on the battlefield. Losing individual models is acceptable. Losing tempo is not.

2

Fragile Discipline

Low Morale means stress thresholds are reached quickly. Insurgent models are prime targets for Stress Exploitation. Leaders are limited and expendable.

3

Tempo Saturation

Large Insurgent forces contribute more Order Cards to the shared deck, creating activation unpredictability. More models means more cards. Use this unpredictability as a tool.

4

Ambush Shock

When an Insurgent Model performs an Ambush Action and makes a Shoot attack: if at least one Hit is rolled (before Saves), the target Model gains +1 additional Stress. Applied even if no damage is inflicted. Does not stack per Hit.

5

IED Network

SETUP

At the end of Game Setup, the Insurgent player places 2 IED tokens and 2 Blank tokens face-down (4 tokens total). Each token must be:

  • At least 3" from the Attacker's Deployment Zone
  • At least 6" from any other token
DETONATING AN IED

Insurgent Model uses Detonate IED Special Action (1 Action). Not a Shoot action. May Shoot in the same activation.

  • Model must be within 24" of the target. No LoS required.
  • Flip the token: Blank = removed, no effect. IED = detonates.
  • Resolve as a standard attack following Section 6.1. Targets roll their Defense Roll as normal.
  • Attack: 4 dice hitting on 5+, Explosive (3"). Cover ignored.
  • Each affected Model gains +1 additional Stress Counter.
  • Each token is one-shot only.

LIST BUILDING

  • 1000 points or fewer.
  • Fire Teams, each led by one Team Leader.
  • Fire Teams must include at least two Models.
  • Fire Teams may only have one specialist of each type.
  • One Cell Leader (Squad Leader) required.
  • One Sniper Team and one AT Team may be included per list.
