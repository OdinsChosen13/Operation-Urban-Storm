CHANGELOG

Version History — V1.1.2 to V1.6.0

Complete record of all changes, additions, balance updates, and design decisions across every version of Operation Urban Storm.

V1.6.0 May 2026 MINOR — NEW FACTION + SITE LAUNCH

V1.6.0 delivers the Ukrainian TDF faction, the full OUS public website, and a suite of balance and rules updates including VDV Assault Tempo, Insurgent Ambush Instinct, the Breach action system, and redesigned Stress Exploitation tiers.

NEW — UKRAINIAN TERRITORIAL DEFENSE FORCES

Faction: Ukrainian TDF — "Hold the Line"

Fourth faction added. Full defensive doctrine built around the Designated Sector — an 8" zone placed blind at Step 6 of setup that grants Move +1", DR -1, and Hit +1 to all friendly models within it. Serzhant may spend a full activation to relocate the token.

Drone Superiority

TDF-exclusive trait. When a TDF Drone Operator's Recon Drone applies ISR (Spotted), cover is reduced to 0 instead of 1. Friendly TDF models attacking a Spotted model gain +1 attack die. TDF lists may include up to 3 Drone Operators; no other faction may field more than 1.

Drone System — Three Configurations

Drone Operator is a two-model unit sharing 2 combined actions. Three drone types available at list building: Recon (SPOT action, ISR/outdoors), Loiter (ATTACK 4D/6+/C-FIRE/EXPL 1, outdoors), FPV Strike (ATTACK 5D/5+/PEN 1/EXPL 1, indoors and outdoors, one-shot). Drone has its own Move, LoS, and DR. Evasive keyword: always treated as Light Cover. Immune to Stress.

TDF Unit Roster Locked

Serzhant (SL), Molodshyi (TL), Strilets (Rifleman), Kulemetnik (MG), Hranatometnik (Grenadier), Strilok (Marksman), Feldcher (Medic), Shturmovyk (CQB Specialist), Protytankist + AT Loader (AT Team), Dronivnyk + Drone (Drone Team — up to 3 per list).

NEW — VDV FACTION TRAIT

Assault Tempo

When a VDV Infantry Model performs a Move action followed immediately by a Shoot action in the same activation, and that Move covers at least 3" toward any enemy Model, it may reroll one failed die on that Shoot action. Does not stack. Vehicles do not benefit.

NEW — INSURGENT FACTION TRAIT

Ambush Instinct

When an Insurgent Model performs an Ambush Shoot action, it may reroll one failed die on that attack. Applies to Ambush Shoot actions only. Does not stack with any other reroll effect.

RULES — WEBSITE BUILD

Public Site Launched — odinschosen13.github.io/Operation-Urban-Storm

Full rules site built and deployed on GitHub Pages. Core Rulebook, all faction doctrine pages (US Army, VDV, Insurgents, TDF), Quickstart Guide, FAQ & Errata, Vehicle Roster reference, and List Builder all published. Battle Deck card images (Objectives and Deployments) added to the List Builder print export as a reference panel.

2v2 Team Rules Added — Section 3.4

Multiplayer 2v2 rules added to Core Rulebook. Each player builds a 500pt list. All four players' Order Tokens share one pool. Both Squad Leaders on the same team operate independently with full abilities. Initiative determined by combined stressed model count per team. Faction abilities do not cross faction lines.

Objective Cards Revised

Hard Rain redesigned as a tug-of-war power track (−2 to +2) across 3 Grid Tokens. Contested Ground added as a zone control objective. Gold Rush and Bad Intel retained with clarification passes. EXTRACT action in Gold Rush clarified — no additional range check required since the model is already carrying the token.

V1.5.0 April–May 2026 MINOR — BREACH SYSTEM + LIST BUILDER

V1.5.0 introduces the Breach action, replaces the legacy CQB building bonus, redesigns Stress Exploitation, expands Rally, formalizes the Active Player card, and delivers the first public list builder web app.

RULES — BREACH ACTION (NEW)

Breach — Section 4.3

The old CQB building bonus (+2 dice inside a building) is replaced by the Breach action — a full forced-entry system for doorway entry into occupied rooms. May only be declared if at least one enemy Model is visible inside upon completing the Move. Participating models consume their Activation or Ambush token. Window entry not permitted for Breach.

Breach Modifiers

CQB Weapon (breaching or defending): +1 die, hit value improved by 1. Non-CQB Weapon: −1 die, hit value worsened by 1. Defender with Ambush declared: additional +1 hit improvement (stacks). Already-activated defender: additional −1 die, hit worsened by 1, must use primary weapon (stacks). Attacks grouped by target pairing, resolve sequentially. Mutual wipe is a valid outcome.

RULES — STRESS EXPLOITATION REDESIGN

New Exploitation Tiers — Section 5.4

All three effects now represent suppression and degraded performance rather than hard action removal. Spend 2: target loses 1 attack die on next Shoot this activation. Spend 3: target's Move halved next activation. Spend 4: target's cover reduced by 1 until end of Turn (minimum 0, Light negated, Heavy becomes Light).

RULES — SQUAD LEADER RALLY REDESIGN

Rally Expanded — Section 7.2

Rally now targets all friendly Models within 3" and LoS, not a single model. Roll a D5 — the result is a pool of Stress counters to distribute freely across any eligible Models within range. Cannot target the SL itself. SL gains +1 Stress regardless of roll. Costs one action.

RULES — ADDITIONAL CHANGES

Ambush Chain — Section 4.3

When an Ambush reaction triggers another Ambush, an Ambush Chain begins. Only one Ambush may be declared and fully resolved at a time. Players alternate until one side declines or has no eligible models. A side that declines is permanently out of the chain.

Stress Threshold Timing — Section 5.1

Stress thresholds are evaluated once, at the start of a Model's activation, before any actions are taken. Stress gained mid-activation (Ambush reactions, Leadership costs, other effects) does not apply restrictions until the start of that Model's next activation.

Active Player Tracking — Section 3.1.4

Active Player card formalized as an official game component. Passed to the player whose turn it is to draw from the Order Deck. Any agreed-upon alternative indicator may be used.

Legal Position Definition — Section 4.3

Legal Position added as a formal definition: model fully within battlefield boundary; not overlapping impassable terrain; not overlapping any other model's base at end of movement; base fully on platform with no overhang when elevated. Models may move through friendly Infantry during movement but may not end overlapping any base. Models may not move through Vehicles.

M203 / Mark Token Clarification

Weapons with the Explosive keyword may not benefit from Mark tokens. Mark bonus applies only to direct single-target attacks.

APP — LIST BUILDER

List Builder Web App — V1.5.0 Launch

Web-based list builder deployed at GitHub Pages. US Army, Insurgents, and VDV factions integrated. Save/Load via localStorage. Print/Export with single-column unit cards and weapon replacement resolution. Vehicle support added (Stryker, Bradley). Fireteam picker modal. Squad Leader enforcement, specialist limits, and independent unit slots all enforced.

V1.4.0 May 2026 MINOR — SPECIALIST UNITS

V1.4.0 introduces five specialist unit classes across all three factions, plus the AT Team independent unit structure. Key fixes include Insurgent AK-47 hit value corrections and IED token count adjustment.

NEW — SPECIALIST UNIT CLASSES

Sniper Team

Two-model Independent Unit (Sniper + Spotter). 8" minimum range. 2" coherency between models. One per list. Operates outside Fireteam structure. Separate Activation Tokens per model. Added to all three factions.

Medic

Field Treatment (1 Action): Select friendly within 3" and LoS. That model removes all Stress. Medic gains +1 Stress. Last Chance (passive): When a friendly within 3" is removed, attempt revival at start of Medic's next activation. Roll 1D10, 8+: returns within 3" with Stress = half Morale rounded up. Added to all three factions.

CQB Specialist

Aggressive Entry and Urban Predator passives. Built around CQB keyword weapons. Added to all three factions.

Marksman

Move 5". Steady Aim (1 Action): Enter Aimed state. Next Shoot gains +1 die and ignores Light Cover. Lost if model moves. Added to all three factions.

AT Team — Section 10.2

Independent Unit: Gunner + Loader. Assisted Reload: if the Loader has not yet activated this turn and is within 2" of the Gunner, the Gunner's RLD costs no action; Loader has one fewer action this turn. Deploy keyword: weapon must be set up before firing; moving after deploy returns to undeployed state. One AT shot per activation. Added to all three factions.

FIXES

Insurgent AK-47 Hit Values Corrected

AK-47 hit values corrected to 7+ across Cell Leader and Team Leader profiles. These were carry-forward errors from legacy draft content that had not been updated to match the Unit Card Master.

Insurgent IED Token Count Adjusted

IED Network reduced from 6 tokens to 4 (2 IED, 2 Blank). Corrects an oversight in the original design — 6 tokens gave too much area coverage for a 3'×3' board.

Kornet AT Team Base Size

Kornet Gunner base size increased to 32mm to reflect the crew-served platform footprint.

V1.3.3 March 2026 PATCH — INITIATIVE + TOKENS + CLIMBING

Redesigns the initiative system from a static Defender-first rule to a dynamic stress-count mechanic from Turn 2 onward. Clarifies Order Card and Activation Token ownership as strict 1:1. Adds explicit climbing rules.

Initiative System Redesign — Section 3.1.1

Turn 1: Defender goes first (unchanged). Turn 2+: Both players count friendly Models carrying one or more Stress Counters. Player with the lower count goes first for both the Give Orders Phase and Model Activation Phase. Tie: previous Turn's initiative holder retains initiative.

Order Cards & Activation Tokens — 1:1 Clarification

Every Model has exactly one dedicated Order Card and one dedicated Activation Token. Always a 1:1 relationship with no exceptions. Squad Activation rule removed from Section 6.2 — it was solving a problem that does not exist under the correct system.

Climbing Rules Added — Section 4.3

Model must begin activation within 1" of the ladder. Full vertical distance must be covered in a single Move action. Model must end in a legal position at the top. If Move value is insufficient to reach the top, the climb cannot be attempted. Partial climbs explicitly prohibited. Elevators do not cost movement.

V1.3.2 March 2026 PATCH — ARC ARMOR + VEHICLE ROSTER

Introduces the Arc Armor system for T2 and T3 vehicles and closes the SPG-9 firing restriction. No existing rules changed — all additions are net-new rules text.

Arc Armor System — Section 9.5

Arc Armor is now a fully defined mechanic. Crosshair Method: two imaginary lines through the vehicle hull (front-to-rear and side-to-side) divide the vehicle into four quadrants. Front quadrant → Front Armor. Either side quadrant → Side Armor. Rear quadrant → Rear Armor. Edge case: attacker exactly on a dividing line chooses which arc applies. Applies to T2 and T3 vehicles. T1 vehicles retain a single flat Armor value.

SPG-9 Front Arc Firing Restriction

The SPG-9 recoilless rifle cannot fire into the vehicle's Front arc. The truck cab physically blocks forward engagement and the weapon's backblast creates a lethal forward zone. Noted on the SPG-9 weapon profile in the Vehicle Roster.

V1.3.1 March 2026 PATCH — RULES CLARIFICATIONS + SMOKE KEYWORD

Resolves rules conflicts, closes ambiguities, differentiates overlapping Leadership abilities, and introduces the Smoke keyword for grenades and grenade launchers. No new systems beyond Smoke.

PASS 1 — RULES CONFLICTS & AMBIGUITIES

CQB Keyword Redefined

CQB redefined as a bonus keyword: +1 die while Engaged (within 1"). +2 dice while inside a building or enclosed structure. If both conditions are met, apply the higher bonus only. Removed redundant "may be used during any Shoot or Ambush action" language.

Action Uniqueness — Section 4.3

Reworded for clarity: "Each action type may only be taken once per activation, except Move which may be taken twice." Removes ambiguity around the word "unique."

Reload Keyword Reworked

Removed auto-reload at end of next activation. New wording: After firing, the weapon may not be used again until the Model spends 1 Action to reload. A Model may reload and Shoot in the same activation. Intent: Turn 1 shoot, Turn 2 reload-then-shoot.

Hard Rain DETONATE Clarification

Added explicit attack resolution instruction: resolve as a standard attack following Section 6.1. Targets roll their Defense Roll as normal. Closes ambiguity on whether DR applies.

Lob Keyword — Cover Interaction

Grenades: Ignore Light Cover. Heavy Cover reduces hits by 2 as normal. Removed "affected by cover" language which conflicted with the Explosive keyword.

PASS 2 — LEADERSHIP DIFFERENTIATION

Lead From The Front — Redesigned as Offensive Push

Select one friendly Fireteam Model within 2" and LoS with Stress ≥ Morale value. That Model may perform two actions this activation regardless of Stress. Stress Counters remain. TL gains +1 Stress. Cannot target TL itself. Lost if TL forced to Hit-the-Dirt this turn.

Rally — Redesigned as Stress Reduction

Select one friendly Model within 3" and LoS. That Model immediately removes Stress Counters down to one below its Morale value. SL gains +1 Stress. Cannot target SL itself. Cannot prevent Excessive Stress already triggered this activation.

One Leadership Ability Per Activation

A Model may only benefit from one Leadership ability per activation. Prevents stacking Lead From The Front and Rally on the same model.

Stress Exploitation — Declaration Timing Clarified

Exploitation must be declared before any movement is measured or dice are rolled for the triggering action. Once the action begins to resolve, the window to declare Exploitation has passed.

PASS 3 — NEW CONTENT

Smoke Keyword Added

When deployed, place a Smoke token at the target point. Projects a 2" radius half-sphere cloud extending 2" horizontally and 2" vertically from the token base. Completely blocks LoS for any line passing through it. Models on elevated positions above 2" may have LoS over the cloud. Removed during Cleanup Phase of the current Turn. Applicable to thrown smoke grenades (Lob) and grenade launcher smoke rounds (Shoot/IDF).

Convoy Vehicle Stat Profile Added — Hard Transit

CEV 3 / Armor 1 / Morale 2. PEN < 1: hit does not resolve but Stress is gained. PEN ≥ 1: resolves normally. Disabled and Destroyed rules added to the card for self-contained play. Move stat removed — Convoy Vehicles use Automatic Advance only.

V1.3.0 March 2026 MINOR — BATTLE CARD SYSTEM

Closes Deferred #4 from V1.2.0. The legacy objective selection process is replaced by a physical Battle Deck system using three card types. New content: 5 Objective Cards, 5 Deployment Cards, 5 Extraction Cards.

NEW SYSTEM — BATTLE DECK

Battle Deck Draft — Section 1.5

Players each build a Battle Deck of 6 cards (3 Objective, 3 Deployment, no duplicates within your own deck; overlap between players permitted). Draft process: Defender chooses whose deck of 3 becomes the active pool. Attacker presents 2 of 3 to the Defender. Defender picks 1. Repeated for Objective and Deployment in that order.

OBJECTIVE CARDS

CASEVAC

Both sides race to collect 3 Casualty Tokens. Attacker escorts to Landing Zone; Defender captures to Detention Marker. Shared PICKUP and PASS escort mechanics. Dropped Token rule applies. VP ceiling 11/11.

HARD RAIN

Attacker plants and detonates charges on 3 Target Tokens. Explosive 4" blast affects both sides. Charge cannot be detonated the same Turn it was planted. DESTROYED tokens cannot be re-armed. Defender disarms or assesses destroyed targets (2 actions, once per token). VP ceiling 11/11.

GOLD RUSH

Both sides race to collect 3 Intel Tokens and extract to own deployment zone. Either player can pick up and escort. Defender may destroy unescorted tokens to deny Attacker. VP ceiling 11/11.

BROKEN ARROW

3 Isolated Models placed deep in Defender's half before deployment. Activate through Order Deck only. Move 3" toward nearest friendly Attacker. Attacker links up and escorts to LZ. Defender engages them. VP ceiling 12/11.

HARD TRANSIT

3 passive Convoy Vehicles enter on Turns 1, 2, and 3. Advance automatically during Cleanup: Unescorted 6", Escorted 12". Disabled vehicles cannot advance. Route blocked by wrecks — vehicles always find a path around. VP ceiling 11/11.

DEPLOYMENT CARDS

BATTLE LINE, FLANKING ADVANCE, ENCIRCLEMENT, AMBUSH, CROSSROADS

Five deployment configurations: standard opposite edges (4" zones), adjacent 90-degree edges, Defender in 10"×10" center vs. Attacker on any edges, Attacker deploys first with Defender split into Main Body and Ambush Group, and opposite corners (8"×8" zones).

RULES FIX

Dropped Token Rule — Section 12.1

When a model drops a token for any reason (destroyed while escorting, failed pass, any other effect), the token is placed in base contact before the model moves or is removed. A dropped token may be picked up by either player on a subsequent activation unless the active Objective Card states otherwise.

V1.1.4 March 2026 PATCH — LEADERSHIP REWORK + STRESS TUNING

Addresses playtest feedback identifying Squad Leaders and Team Leaders as underutilized. Root cause: Stress not generating enough meaningful decisions. Introduces Stress Exploitation, new Leadership abilities, and updated faction doctrines.

Stress Removal — 8+ → 7+

Stress Counter removal roll changed from 8+ to 7+ (30% → 40% chance per die). Models can now manage light stress independently. Heavy stress still requires leadership. Creates a clearer gradient: light (manageable), moderate (requires attention), excessive (crisis).

Stress Exploitation System — New

Reactive currency system. When enemy declares an action, opposing player may declare Exploitation before any movement or dice. Spend 2 Stress: −1 attack die. Spend 3: lose one action. Spend 4: forced Hit-the-Dirt. Once per Model per activation. No LoS required. Spent Stress removed.

Squad Leader Updates

Orders now require Line of Sight (3" range unchanged, orders still free). New Rally ability: costs one action, targets one friendly within 3" and LoS with Stress ≥ Morale — that model performs two actions this activation regardless of Stress (Stress Counters remain). Authority Override clarification: SL gains the Stress that would have triggered the forced Hit-the-Dirt.

Team Leader Updates

Command Range defined at 2". New Passive — Aggression: all friendly Fireteam Models within 2" of TL gain +1 attack die on Shoot actions. New Active — Lead From The Front (once per turn): target stressed Fireteam Model within 2" and LoS performs two actions this activation. Stress Transfer updated to interact with new Exploitation system.

Insurgent Doctrine Update

Fighter Order restriction added — Fighters cannot receive Orders from any Leader (activates through deck draw only, eliminates blobbing). Fighter Morale changed from 1 to 2. TL Emergency Command added for when Cell Leader is removed. New Specialist: AK Veteran Fighter (orderable mid-range infantry).

US Army — Coordinated Fire Added

When a US Army Model makes a Shoot action, place a Marked Token on the target. A friendly US Army Model that shoots at a Marked target may spend the token to gain +1 attack die. Tokens removed during Cleanup. US Army exclusive. Fireteam Cohesion and Coordinated Fire can stack — intentional.

V1.1.3 March 8, 2026 PATCH — RULES PASS + COVER REWORK + IED REWRITE

First formal rules pass. Addresses inconsistencies, ambiguous language, and balance issues. Most significant changes: full rewrite of the attack sequence and cover system (stress now generates from hits), complete IED rules rewrite, and HP → CEV standardization across all units.

HP → CEV Standardization

All instances of "HP" (US forces) and "X hp" (Insurgent forces) replaced with "CEV" (Combat Effectiveness Value) throughout all documents. 10 replacements total.

Cover System Rework

Cover previously reduced the attacker's dice pool before rolling. Now reduces hits after rolling. Light Cover: was "remove 2 dice from pool," now "remove 1 hit after the attack roll." Heavy Cover: was "remove 3 dice," now "remove 2 hits." Attackers always roll their full pool.

Stress from Hits — New Rule

Defender now gains Stress equal to the number of hits scored after the attack roll, in addition to 1 Stress for being targeted. This Stress is assigned before cover removes hits. Cover reduces wounds but does not reduce Stress.

Attack Sequence Rewritten — Section 6.1

New step order: (1) Determine LoS, (2) Determine Cover (note level only), (3) Attack Roll (full pool), (4) Count Hits, (5) Assign Stress (before cover), (6) Apply Cover (−1/−2 hits), (7) Defense Roll, (8) Wound Allocation.

IED Network Rewrite

Detonate IED is now a Special Action — does not consume Shoot action; Insurgent may Detonate and Shoot in the same activation. Attack roll changed from 1D10 (hits on 6+) to 4 dice hitting on 5+. Removed flat 2 Stress to all models; replaced by standard hit-based Stress plus +1 additional Stress per affected model. Cover explicitly ignored.

Ambush Action List Updated

Added "or a Special Action that explicitly states it may be used during an Ambush reaction" to the valid Ambush action list. IED detonation during Ambush no longer contradicts Ambush rules.

Disembark Restriction Removed

Removed restriction preventing Move during the same activation as Disembark. Disembark now costs 1 action with no restrictions on remaining actions.

Stress Threshold Clarification

When both Stress ≥ Morale (1 action) and Stress ≥ 2× Morale (Hit-the-Dirt) thresholds are met, the 2× threshold supersedes the reduced-action rule entirely.

VDV Command Imperative Clarified

Rewritten to clarify the Squad Leader spends 1 of its own Actions to issue the command. Target Model performs its granted Action immediately and fully before the Squad Leader continues. Added explicit sequencing. Operates on the Squad Leader's action economy, not the target's activation.

Unit Roster Moved to Unit Card Master

The model roster has been moved to the OUS Unit Card Master document (the authoritative source for all unit stats). The rulebook roster section is reference-only legacy content. All stat and points conflicts default to the Unit Card Master.
