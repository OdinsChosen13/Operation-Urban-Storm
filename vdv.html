FACTION OVERVIEW

Russian VDV forces are elite assault infantry designed to seize key terrain rapidly through decisive action and centralized command authority. VDV represents a high ceiling, low floor faction. Played well, they are aggressive and hard to suppress. Played poorly — or when their Squad Leader falls — they collapse fast.

FACTION TRAITS

1

Assault Mobility

VDV Infantry do not carry the Encumbered (ENC) keyword on any weapons.

Exception: The Kornet AT Team Gunner carries ENC. Assault Mobility does not suppress ENC on crew-served platforms.
2

Command Imperative Once per Turn

During the VDV Squad Leader's activation: the Squad Leader spends 1 Action to grant one friendly Infantry Model within Command Range 1 immediate Action. This does not count as the target Model's activation. The Squad Leader gains +1 Stress. The granted Action cannot remove Stress.

3

Coercive Authority

  • Authority Override costs the Squad Leader +2 Stress instead of +1.
  • Rally costs the Squad Leader +2 Stress instead of +1.

⚠ Command Failure — Command Collapse

When a VDV Squad Leader is removed from play: all friendly Infantry Models with LoS within 6" immediately gain +1 Stress.

LIST BUILDING

  • 1000 points or fewer.
  • Fire Teams, each led by one Team Leader.
  • Fire Teams must include at least two Models.
  • Fire Teams may only have one specialist of each type.
  • One Squad Leader (Leytenant) required.
  • One Sniper Team and one AT Team may be included per list.
