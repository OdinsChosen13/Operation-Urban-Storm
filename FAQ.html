FAQ & ERRATA

V1.6.0

Frequently asked questions, rules clarifications, and official corrections for Operation Urban Storm.

STRESS & MORALE

Q: Does Stress from hits apply before or after cover?
Stress is always applied before cover removes hits. Cover reduces hits that proceed to Defense rolls, but does not reduce the Stress generated by those hits. (See Section 5.2 and 6.1.5)
Q: When exactly are Stress thresholds checked?
Stress thresholds are evaluated once, at the start of a Model's activation, before any actions are taken. Stress gained mid-activation — from Ambush reactions, Leadership costs, or other effects — does not apply restrictions until the start of that Model's next activation. (See Section 5.1)
Q: Can a TL use Lead From The Front on themselves?
No. Lead From The Front cannot target the TL itself. (See Section 7.1)
Q: Does the SL gain Stress even if Rally removes zero counters?
Yes. The Squad Leader gains +1 Stress regardless of the D5 roll result. (See Section 7.2)

ATTACKS & COMBAT

Q: Does cover reduce my dice pool?
No. Cover never reduces the attacker's dice pool. Cover only removes hits after the attack roll, at Step 6 of the attack sequence. (See Section 4.2 and 6.1.2)
Q: Can an Engaged model shoot with a non-CQB weapon?
No. Engaged Models (within 1" of an enemy) may only Shoot with CQB keyword weapons. (See Section 4.3 — Engaged Models)
Q: When can Stress Exploitation be declared?
Exploitation must be declared before any movement is measured or dice are rolled for the triggering action. Once the action begins to resolve, the window has passed. (See Section 5.4)
Q: Can a model Ambush if it already performed a Shoot action this activation?
No. A Model may not declare Ambush if it has already performed a Shoot action this activation. (See Section 4.3 — Ambush)

BREACH

Q: Can models enter through windows during a Breach?
No. Window entry is not permitted for a Breach. Breach must be executed through a doorway. (See Section 4.3 — Breach)
Q: Can a model whose Order Card hasn't been drawn join a Breach?
No. Models whose Order Card has not yet been drawn may not join a Breach as attackers. (See Section 4.3 — Breach)
Q: Is a mutual wipe during a Breach a legal outcome?
Yes. A mutual wipe is a valid outcome. There are no survivors and the room is considered empty. (See Section 4.3 — Breach)
Q: Can a Model Disembark and Breach in the same activation?
Yes. Disembark costs one action; the Breach Move through the doorway is the second action. A single Model may Disembark and then initiate or join a Breach in the same activation, provided its Breach Move can legally reach the doorway from its disembark point and at least one enemy Model is visible inside on completing the Move. The Breach exchange is the attack — the Model has no remaining action for a separate Shoot. (See Section 4.3 — Embark / Disembark and Breach)
Q: Can a full squad Disembark from a vehicle and Breach together in one activation?
No. Only one Model activates at a time, and still-embarked Models cannot be nominated as Breach participants — a Model must already have Disembarked and be staged in a legal position within 1" of the entry point (holding a face-up Activation token or declared Ambush) to join. Additional breachers must therefore have Disembarked on prior activations. This permits a staged mounted assault while preventing a whole squad from pouring out of the hull into a room in a single activation. (See Section 4.3 — Breach and Embark / Disembark)

VEHICLES

Q: Do vehicles benefit from Squad Leader or Team Leader abilities?
No, unless explicitly stated. Vehicles do not benefit from SL or TL abilities by default. (See Section 7.3)
Q: Can a vehicle move forward and reverse in the same activation?
No. A vehicle may not move forward and reverse in the same activation. (See Section 4.3 — Move Vehicles)
Q: What happens when a vehicle reaches CEV 0?
The vehicle is destroyed and remains as impassable wreckage. Then roll an Explosion Check: 1D10 — on 6+ it explodes, and you roll 1D10 for each embarked model (on 7+: suffers a hit). All embarked models placed after destruction gain 2 Stress Counters. (See Section 9.4)

LIST BUILDING

Q: Is there a limit on the number of Fire Teams?
No. There is no limit on the number of Fire Teams in a list, as long as the total is 1000 points or fewer. (See Section 8.1)
Q: Can both players include the same Battle Deck card?
Yes. Both players may include the same card in their respective decks. There is no restriction on card overlap between players. (See Section 1.4)
