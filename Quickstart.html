GAME BASICS

Battlefield

3' × 3' board
40% Heavy Cover
30% Light Cover

Game Length

5 Turns
1000pts per list
60-90 min play time

Dice

D10 for all rolls
D5: roll D10
1-2=1, 3-4=2, 5-6=3
7-8=4, 9-10=5

Measurements

All in inches (")
Measure base-to-base
Measure anytime

TURN STRUCTURE

1

GIVE ORDERS PHASE

  • Initiative player issues orders first
  • Squad Leader orders models within 3" + LoS
  • Place Order Tokens next to ordered models
  • Shuffle remaining tokens into shared deck
↓
2

MODEL ACTIVATION PHASE

  • Initiative player takes first activation
  • Players alternate activations
  • Activate ordered model OR draw from deck
  • Perform up to 2 actions per activation
↓
3

CLEANUP PHASE

  • Return all Order Tokens
  • Remove temp tokens (Marked, etc.)
  • Stress and Wounds remain
  • Convoy advance (if applicable)

INITIATIVE (Turn 2+)

Count models with 1+ Stress. Lower total goes first. Tied = previous initiative player retains.

ACTIONS

MOVE

Move up to MOV stat. May take twice per activation. Infantry move through friendlies. Cannot end overlapping bases.

SHOOT

Attack with one weapon. Roll dice pool, count hits, assign stress, apply cover, defender rolls saves.

OBJECTIVE ACTION

Perform action defined by active Objective Card. Once per activation.

SIGHT-IN

Remain stationary. Reroll up to 2 dice when Shooting this activation.

HIT-THE-DIRT

Gain Light Cover until end of Turn. Remove D5 Stress. Only action this activation.

AMBUSH

React at start/end of enemy action. Once per Turn per model. Move, Shoot, Hit-the-Dirt, or Special.

BREACH

Coordinated forced entry through doorway. CQB weapons get +1 die, +1 hit value. Non-CQB get -1 die, -1 hit.

EMBARK / DISEMBARK

Enter/exit vehicle within 1". Cannot do both in same activation.

ACTION RESTRICTIONS

Each action type once per activation (except Move, which can be taken twice).
Stress ≥ Morale: Only 1 action this activation.
Stress ≥ 2× Morale: Forced Hit-the-Dirt (Excessive Stress).

ENGAGED MODELS (Within 1")

May only Shoot with CQB weapons while Engaged.

COMBAT RESOLUTION

1

Line of Sight

True LoS. If any part visible (excluding base), LoS exists.

2

Determine Cover

50%+ obscured = cover. Does NOT reduce dice pool.

3

Attack Roll

Roll full dice pool from weapon profile.

4

Count Hits

Dice ≥ Hit value = hits.

5

Assign Stress

+1 for being targeted + hits total. BEFORE cover.

6

Apply Cover

Light: -1 hit. Heavy: -2 hits. Min 0.

7

Defense Roll

Per remaining hit: ≥ DR = negated. Lower = wound.

8

Wounds

CEV -1 per wound. CEV 0 = removed.

STRESS & MORALE

Stress < Morale

FULL ACTIONS

Model acts normally. 2 actions per activation.

Stress ≥ Morale

REDUCED ACTIONS

Only 1 action this activation.

Stress ≥ 2× Morale

EXCESSIVE STRESS

Forced Hit-the-Dirt. Activation ends.

STRESS REMOVAL (At Activation Start)

Roll 1D10 per Stress Counter. On 7, 8, 9, or 10: remove 1 Stress.

STRESS EXPLOITATION

Spend Stress as currency when enemy declares an action. Declare before movement/dice. Once per model per activation.

Stress Spent Effect
2 Target loses 1 attack die on next Shoot
3 Target's Move halved next activation
4 Target's cover reduced by 1 until end of Turn

TEAM LEADER INTERCEPTION

When Exploitation declared against Fireteam model within 2" of TL: TL may intercept. Stress transfers to TL. Effect negated.

LEADERSHIP

Leadership redistributes, delays, or pushes through Stress at a cost. One Leadership ability per model per activation.

TEAM LEADER (2" Range)

Aggression (Passive)

Fireteam models within 2" gain +1 attack die on Shoot actions.

Lead From The Front (Once/Turn)

Target stressed Fireteam model within 2" acts at full actions regardless of Stress. TL gains +1 Stress.

Stress Transfer

Intercept Exploitation on Fireteam model within 2". Stress transfers to TL. Effect negated.

SQUAD LEADER (3" Range)

Orders

During Give Orders Phase, issue orders to models within 3" + LoS. Free action.

Rally (1 Action)

Roll D5. Distribute removals across friendlies within 3". SL gains +1 Stress.

Authority Override (Once/Turn)

Prevent forced Hit-the-Dirt on friendly within range. SL absorbs the Stress.

VDV COERCIVE AUTHORITY

VDV Rally and Authority Override cost +2 Stress instead of +1.

VDV COMMAND IMPERATIVE

Once per Turn: SL spends 1 Action to grant friendly model within 3" 1 immediate action. SL gains +1 Stress.

VDV COMMAND COLLAPSE

When VDV SL dies: all friendly Infantry within 6" + LoS gain +1 Stress immediately.

KEYWORD GLOSSARY

CQB (Close Quarters Combat)

+1 die while Engaged. Only CQB weapons can Shoot while Engaged.

C-FIRE (Covering Fire)

Target gains +1 Stress. Friendlies within 2" of target gain +1 Stress if attacker has LoS.

ENC (Encumbered)

Move -2" (min 4").

RLD (Reload)

After firing, spend 1 Action to reload. May reload + Shoot same activation.

EXPL X (Explosive)

X" radius. All models within resolve separately. Ignores cover.

LOB (Lob Grenade)

Throw up to 4". Roll D10: 7+ detonates. 3D / 7+ / 2" radius. Ignores Light Cover.

IDF (Indirect Fire)

Attack without LoS. Roll 10+ to hit. Improves by 1 each miss (min 2). Resets if target moves.

DEPLOY

Spend 1 Action to deploy before firing. Moving undeploys.

ONE-SHOT

After firing, unusable rest of game.

SMOKE

2" radius half-sphere. Blocks LoS. Removed at Cleanup.

CONCEALMENT

Treat Light as Heavy. Heavy removes 3 hits instead of 2.

INJURED

CEV ≤ half starting: Move -3", -1 attack die (min 1).

PEN X (Penetration)

vs Vehicles: PEN < Armor = no effect. PEN ≥ Armor = resolves normally.

ASSISTED RELOAD

If Loader unactivated + within 2" of Gunner: RLD costs no action. Loader has -1 action.

FACTION TRAITS

U.S. ARMY — "Fireteam Cohesion"

Fireteam Cohesion

When within 2" of another Fireteam model: reroll 1 failed die on Move or Shoot.

Coordinated Fire

When Shooting: place Marked Token on target. Friendlies shooting Marked target may spend token for +1 attack die. Removed at Cleanup.

VDV (RUSSIAN AIRBORNE) — "Forced Momentum"

Assault Mobility

VDV Infantry do not carry ENC on any weapons (except Kornet Gunner).

Command Imperative

Once per Turn: SL spends 1 Action to grant friendly model 1 immediate action. SL gains +1 Stress.

Coercive Authority

Rally and Authority Override cost SL +2 Stress instead of +1.

Command Collapse

When SL dies: all friendly Infantry within 6" + LoS gain +1 Stress.

INSURGENTS — "Pressure & Fragmentation"

Asymmetric Force Ratio

Cheapest models in game. Outnumber opponents. Losing models acceptable — losing tempo is not.

Tempo Saturation

More models = more Order Cards in shared deck. Use unpredictability as a weapon.

Ambush Shock

When Ambush Shoot scores 1+ hits: target gains +1 additional Stress (even if no damage).

IED Network

Place 2 IED + 2 Blank tokens face-down during setup. 3" from Attacker zone, 6" apart. Detonate: 1 Action, 24" range, no LoS. 4D / 5+ / EXPL 3" / +1 Stress.

VEHICLES

Movement

Measure from hull center. Tape may bend. Hull must fit through spaces. May Move twice. Reverse at half Move.

Damage

PEN < Armor: no effect. PEN ≥ Armor: resolves normally. Gain 1 Stress when targeted.

Disabled

Stress ≥ 2× Morale: Disabled. Cannot Move, cannot OBJACT, Shoot at -1 die.

Recovery

At activation start: roll D10. 8-10: remove all Stress, no longer Disabled.

Destruction

CEV 0: destroyed, impassable wreckage. Roll D10: 6+ explodes. Embarked models roll D10: 7+ = hit. All survivors gain +2 Stress.

Arc Armor (F/S/R)

Front/Side/Rear armor values. Crosshair method: draw lines lengthwise + widthwise. Attacker's quadrant determines arc.
