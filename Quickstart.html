QUICKSTART GUIDE

V1.6.0

Keep this page open during your match. All essential rules at a glance.

GAME AT A GLANCE

Battlefield3' × 3'
Game Length5 Turns
Point Limit1000 pts
DiceD10
For D5Roll D10: 1–2=1, 3–4=2, 5–6=3, 7–8=4, 9–10=5

TURN STRUCTURE

  1. Give Orders Phase — Player with initiative issues SL Orders first (within 3" + LoS). Remaining tokens shuffled into shared Order Deck.
  2. Model Activation Phase — Players alternate. Activate a Model with a face-up token OR draw from the Order Deck.
  3. Cleanup Phase — Return Order Tokens. Remove temp tokens (Marked, Smoke). Stress & Wounds remain.

INITIATIVE

  • Turn 1: Defender goes first.
  • Turn 2+: Count stressed models (1+ Stress Counter each). Lower count goes first.
  • Tie: Previous initiative holder retains it.

ATTACK SEQUENCE

StepAction
1Determine Line of Sight — crouch behind model, look from head height. Any part visible = LoS.
2Determine Cover — 50%+ obscured = cover. Does NOT reduce dice pool.
3Attack Roll — roll full dice pool from Loadout Card.
4Count Hits — dice equal to or above Hit value.
5Assign Stress — 1 for being targeted + 1 per hit. Applied BEFORE cover.
6Apply Cover — Light: remove 1 hit. Heavy: remove 2 hits. Minimum 0.
7Defense Roll — per remaining hit. Roll vs DR. Equal/above: negated. Below: wound.
8Wound Allocation — CEV -1 per wound. CEV 0: model removed.

ACTIONS (2 per activation)

  • Move — up to Move value. May be taken twice.
  • Shoot — one weapon. Once per activation.
  • Sight-In — stationary; re-roll up to 2 dice this activation.
  • Hit-the-Dirt — only action this activation. Gain Light Cover. Remove D5 Stress.
  • Ambush — react to enemy action. Move, Shoot, or Hit-the-Dirt.
  • OBJACT — per Objective Card. Once per activation.
  • Embark / Disembark — enter/exit vehicle within 1". Not both same activation.

STRESS & MORALE

  • Gain 1 Stress when targeted.
  • Gain +1 Stress per hit (before cover).
  • At activation start: roll 1D10 per Stress. 7+: remove one.
Stress LevelEffect
Stress >= Morale1 action only
Stress >= 2× MoraleForced Hit-the-Dirt, activation ends

STRESS EXPLOITATION

Declare before enemy action resolves. Spend Stress from the targeted model.

SpendEffect
2Target loses 1 attack die this activation
3Target's Move halved next activation
4Target's cover reduced by 1 until end of Turn

TL within 2" may intercept — absorbs stress, negates effect.

VEHICLES

  • 2 actions per activation: Move, Shoot, Special.
  • Hit only if PEN >= Armor Rating.
  • Gain 1 Stress when targeted.
  • Disabled (Stress >= 2× Morale): cannot Move, -1 attack die.
  • Recovery: roll 1D10 at start of activation. 8+: clear all Stress.
  • Destruction: CEV 0 = wreckage. Roll 1D10 — 6+: explodes.

COVER & LINE OF SIGHT

  • LoS: True LoS — crouch behind model, look from head height.
  • Cover: Model is 50%+ obscured from attacker's view.
  • Light Cover: Remove 1 hit (after attack roll).
  • Heavy Cover: Remove 2 hits (after attack roll).
  • Cover does NOT reduce dice pool.
  • Stress from hits is applied BEFORE cover removes hits.

BREACH QUICK RULES

  • Requires at least one enemy visible inside upon completing Move.
  • Through doorways only — no window entry.
  • CQB weapon: +1 die, hit improved by 1.
  • Non-CQB weapon: -1 die, hit worsened by 1.
  • Defender with Ambush: additional +1 hit improvement.
  • Already-activated defender: -1 die, +1 hit worsened, primary weapon only.

VICTORY

  • Highest Victory Points at end of Turn 5 wins.
  • VP earned per active Objective Card.
  • Tie: most models alive wins.
  • Still tied: draw.
